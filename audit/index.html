THE $20M FUNNEL AUDIT PLAYBOOK

20 Critical Conversion Leaks Costing You 6-7 Figures Annually

SECTION 1: TRAFFIC & TRACKING FOUNDATION

The "Am I Flying Blind?" Test

□ 1. Conversion Tracking Accuracy

Are you tracking conversions with both pixel AND server-side tracking (CAPI)?

Why it matters: iOS 14+ blocks 30-40% of pixel data. If you're not seeing this, you're optimizing blind.

Money leak: $50k/month in ads = $15k/month wasted on ghost traffic

Real example: Client was "scaling" an audience with 8x ROAS. Turned on CAPI. Actual ROAS was 2.1x. We killed it and saved $40k/month.

□ 2. Attribution Window Mapping

Do you know your actual customer journey length (first touch to purchase)?

Why it matters: If your sales cycle is 14 days but you're only tracking 7-day attribution, you're killing winners.

Money leak: Cutting profitable campaigns = 30-50% higher CAC

Real example: Client's "best" audience had a 21-day decision window. We extended attribution and found 40% more conversions we were missing.

□ 3. The "Trojan Horse Landing Page"

Are you using a free diagnostic tool/calculator/assessment instead of direct webinar registration?

Why it matters: People love self-assessment. Gets them invested before they even register. Cuts through ad fatigue completely.

Money leak: Direct webinar pages convert at 15-20%. Calculator-first converts at 40-50%.

Real example: Client replaced webinar registration with "Funnel Profitability Calculator." Conversion rate went from 18% to 47% because people wanted their score. 80% of calculator completers registered for webinar.

SECTION 2: LANDING PAGE CONVERSION KILLERS

The "First Impression" Test

□ 4. Above-the-Fold Clarity

Can someone articulate your unique mechanism in 3 seconds?

Why it matters: 53% of mobile users abandon unclear pages immediately.

Money leak: 40% bounce rate improvement = 40% more leads at same ad spend

Real example: Changed headline from "Master Your Business Operations" to "The 90-Day Operator Blueprint: How 7-Figure Entrepreneurs Eliminate Chaos Without Hiring a COO" — CPL dropped 38%.

□ 5. The "Competitor Shadow Campaign"

Are you running Google Search ads on competitor brand names + "review" or "alternative"?

Why it matters: You're catching people in active buying mode who are researching your competitor. Conversion rates are 3-5x higher than cold traffic.

Money leak: Ignoring competitor search traffic = missing your hottest leads at lowest CAC

Real example: Client was spending $80k/month on cold Facebook ads. We added $15k/month on competitor search terms. Those leads closed at $197 CPA vs $420 CPA for FB cold traffic. Same budget, 2x more customers.

□ 6. The "Breakup Email" Dead Lead Reactivation

For people who registered but didn't show or didn't buy, are you sending a "breakup email" 30 days later?

Why it matters: Re-engages dead leads by giving them an out. The ones who reply are HOT and often convert at 2-3x normal rates because they self-select back in.

Money leak: Dead lists with 10,000+ leads = $200k-500k in dormant revenue

Real example: Client had 40,000 dead webinar registrants. Sent breakup email: "It's been a month. I'm guessing this isn't a priority. Unsubscribe or reply if you want one more shot." 380 replied wanting "one more shot." Booked 90 on calls. Closed 31 deals = $147k from dead list.

□ 7. CTA Friction Audit

How many clicks/fields between landing and registration?

Why it matters: Every additional field drops conversions 10-15%. Every extra click loses 25%.

Money leak: Reducing 7 fields to 3 = 2x more registrations

Real example: Client had a 2-step registration (email → form page with 8 fields). We made it 1-step with 3 fields. Reg rate went from 18% to 44%.

SECTION 3: WEBINAR REGISTRATION → SHOW-UP RATE

The "Commitment Gap"

□ 8. Confirmation Page Indoctrination

Does your thank-you page pre-sell the webinar outcome and build anticipation?

Why it matters: A strategic confirmation page increases show-up rates from 25% to 45%+.

Money leak: 20% more attendees = $40k extra revenue per webinar (at 1000 reg)

Real example: Added a 90-second video on confirmation page showing "the 3 myths I'm going to destroy on this webinar." Show-up rate went from 26% to 47%.

□ 9. Reminder Sequence Psychology

Are you sending 5+ reminders with escalating urgency and specificity?

Why it matters: Most people register and forget. Your sequence determines if they show up.

Money leak: Poor sequence = 25% show rate. Optimized = 50%+ (2x revenue per webinar)

Real example: Client was sending 3 generic reminders. We built 7 emails that revealed "what you'll learn" progressively. Show rate jumped from 29% to 51%.

□ 10. The "LinkedIn DM → Webinar Bridge"

Are you using LinkedIn ads to a lead magnet, then having someone send personalized connection + DM invites to an exclusive session?

Why it matters: LinkedIn DMs have 10x higher engagement than email. Personal invite feels VIP. Show-up rates are 60-75% vs 25-35% for email.

Money leak: Email-only webinar invites = 70% of registrants never show up

Real example: Client was running LinkedIn ads straight to webinar reg (22% conversion, 31% show-up). We added lead magnet → manual DM sequence. Show-up rate jumped to 68%. Close rate went from 9% to 14% because attendees felt personally invited.

SECTION 4: WEBINAR CONVERSION ARCHITECTURE

The "Pitch Mechanics"

□ 11. The Big Mechanism

Do you have ONE unique mechanism that's different from competitors?

Why it matters: "Better course on Facebook ads" converts at 3%. "The 7-Figure Ad Account Blueprint" converts at 12%.

Money leak: 4x close rate difference = $120k vs $30k per webinar

Real example: Client was teaching "how to systematize." We reframed it as "The Operator Blueprint: 4 Systems That Run Your Business Without You." Close rate went from 3.8% to 11.2%.

□ 12. Future-Pacing & Belief Stacking

Are you showing them their life AFTER using your method (not just teaching)?

Why it matters: Education-only webinars convert at 2-4%. Future-paced webinars convert at 8-15%.

Money leak: Tripling close rate = 3x revenue per traffic dollar

Real example: Client spent 60 mins teaching, 15 mins pitching. We flipped it: 20 mins teaching, 55 mins showing transformations + what their life looks like after. Close rate tripled.

□ 13. Price Anchoring & Stack

Do you reveal price AFTER building perceived value to 10x the ask?

Why it matters: Revealing $2k before value = 3% close. After proper stack = 12%.

Money leak: $90k vs $360k revenue per 1000 attendees

Real example: Client was revealing price at minute 40. We moved it to minute 68 after stacking $47k in perceived value for a $3,997 offer. Close rate went from 4.1% to 13.7%.

□ 14. Urgency Architecture

Is your deadline real, specific, and tied to a legitimate reason?

Why it matters: Fake scarcity destroys trust. Real scarcity (cohort starts, bonus expires) converts 3-5x higher.

Money leak: Weak urgency = 50% of sales lost to "I'll think about it"

Real example: Changed from "sale ends Friday" to "Cohort 12 starts Monday with live kickoff call. If you miss it, next cohort is 8 weeks away." Immediate purchases jumped 67%.

SECTION 5: POST-WEBINAR CONVERSION SEQUENCE

The "Ghost Money"

□ 15. Replay Strategy

Are no-shows getting a replay with SHORTER deadline than live attendees?

Why it matters: 30-40% of revenue comes from replay viewers IF sequenced correctly.

Money leak: Ignoring replays = leaving $60k+ on table per webinar

Real example: Client wasn't sending replays. We built a 48-hour replay sequence. Added $127k/month from people who registered but didn't show live.

□ 16. The "3-2-1 Countdown Reveal" Post-Webinar Sequence

Are you breaking your offer reveal into 3 sequential emails over 48 hours instead of one big pitch email?

Why it matters: Most people send everything at once. Splitting creates 3 touch points and lets you segment based on who opens what. Fence-sitters need multiple touches.

Money leak: One-email approach = 65% of potential buyers never see your offer

Real example: Client was sending one post-webinar email with price + stack + bonuses (8% conversion). We split into Email 1: "Here's the price," Email 2: "Here's what you get," Email 3: "Bonuses expire in 24 hours." Backend conversion went from 8% to 23% because fence-sitters got multiple decision points.

□ 17. Application Funnel Recovery

If using applications, are you calling no-shows within 2 hours?

Why it matters: Speed to lead determines close rate. <2 hours = 60% contact rate. >24 hours = 15%.

Money leak: 4x more conversations = 4x more high-ticket sales

Real example: Client was calling app submissions once/day in batches. We implemented real-time calling within 30 mins. Contact rate went from 22% to 64%. Close rate jumped accordingly.

SECTION 6: FUNNEL ECONOMICS & BOTTLENECK DIAGNOSIS

The "CFO Test"

□ 18. Unit Economics Mapping

Do you know your numbers at EVERY stage (CPM, CPC, CPL, cost per registrant, cost per attendee, CPA, LTV)?

Why it matters: If you don't know where you're bleeding, you can't fix it. Most funnels have 1-2 major leaks killing ROI.

Money leak: Fixing the #1 bottleneck typically unlocks 40-100% more profit

Real example: Client thought their problem was ad creative (CPL was $84). Real problem was 23% show-up rate. Fixed that first, unlocked $2.5M+/year.

□ 19. Comparative Cohort Analysis

Are you comparing traffic sources, offers, and audiences weekly?

Why it matters: What you're scaling might be your worst performer. Best audience often gets 10x ROI vs worst.

Money leak: Scaling wrong audience = burning $50k+/month in negative ROI

Real example: Client was scaling "entrepreneurs 30-50" broadly. We tested 8 sub-segments. Found "female service providers $1-3M revenue" had 9.2x ROAS vs 1.8x average. Shifted all budget there.

□ 20. LTV Optimization Post-Purchase

Are you tracking and optimizing 90-day retention, upsells, and ascension?

Why it matters: A customer worth $2k vs $6k changes EVERYTHING. You can pay 3x more to acquire them.

Money leak: Ignoring backend = leaving 60-70% of lifetime value on table

Real example: Client had $3k front-end offer, nothing behind it. We built a $12k/year continuity + $25k mastermind ascension path. LTV went from $3,100 to $11,400. Completely changed their acquisition math.
